Designing and developing experiences

I’m a front-end developer, interaction designer and creative technologist. I’ve worked on everything from websites, web-apps, interactive installations to e-learning courses.

Experience and education

Currently I work as a web developer and designer for Omega 365, and I have also done UI design, information architecture among other things for them in the past.

I’ve also worked for Back where I designed and programmed interactive installations, games and other interactive experiences for museums and visitation centers. I also created temporary interactive installations for Oslo Children’s Museum. You can get more details on what I worked in my portfolio.

For my bachelor I studied at the University of Oslo, with a focus on interaction design, informatics and media theory. I previously studied interaction design at Kristiania University College.
