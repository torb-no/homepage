Work

Currently I work as a web designer and developer for Omega 365, and I have also done UI design, information architecture among other things for them in the past.

I’ve also worked for Back where I designed and programmed interactive installations, games and other interactive experiences. I have also created temporary interactive installations for Oslo Children’s Museum. You can get more details on what I worked in my portfolio

For my bachelor I studied at the University of Oslo, with a focus on interaction design, informatics and media theory. I previously studied interaction design at Kristiania University College.
